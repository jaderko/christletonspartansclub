About the Club

We are students running a local basketball club in Christleton. Our mission is to build a diverse, welcoming community that engages and connects people of all ages through basketball.

Sessions & Fixtures

We run shoot-arounds, open games and training during the school holidays, especially on Wednesdays.

Our Kit

The Christleton Spartans jersey features a black base with turquoise trim, a bold Spartan crest on the right chest, and large turquoise numbers in a stylised Spartan font. It represents integrity, strength and togetherness.

To order, DM us on Instagram.

Our Mascot

Our Spartan mascot embodies the strength, unity and determination of our club. More than just a figurehead, the Spartan attends training sessions, encourages new players and symbolises our commitment to teamwork and resilience.

Important Information

This is a student-led community basketball club. We are not responsible for the supervision or safety of children at any session.

All participants under 17 must be accompanied by a responsible adult.
